Pets up for adoption

Bringing a pet into your home is an important decision which should be thoroughly considered and prepared, requiring agreement of all family members. The family should consider the time and money spent on pets and provide suitable conditions for the animal. If the family has agreed in these questions, decision should be made about the type of animal suitable for the family and the family should consider the possibility to provide a home for one of the hundreds of cats and dogs living in the animal shelters or in foster-homes of non-governmental organizations. If there is more time and wish to commit, the cat in need of help can be saved right from the street. For instructions, any non-governmental organization can be addressed.
